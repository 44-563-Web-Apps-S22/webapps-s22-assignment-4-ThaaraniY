Tartuffe

Act SCENE IV Scene between Tartuffe,Elmire

Tartuffe

May Heaven forever in its great bounty Grant you good health both in soul and body, And bless your days as much as he desires Who is the humblest of those your love inspires!


Elmire

I'm much obliged for your pious wishes, but please,

Tartuffe

Let us be seated and put ourselves at ease. [sitting down]. Have you quite recovered from your illness?
